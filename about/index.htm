About

DiscreteLogics creates Teatime, a set of tools for time series storage, visualization and analysis. Highest possible performance leads design, followed by well designed programming models and user interfaces. Small tools are easy to evaluate and integrate with existing custom applications or tools like R, Octave/Matlab or Python. Being operating system and programming language agnostic is the goal.

Team

Thomas Hulka, Founder

Thomas has 15 years experience in software engineering specialising in high performance computing, object oriented modeling and user interface design. Before founding Discretelogics, he worked for a derivative trading company. He holds a Master's degree in computer science from Stanford University, an Engineering degree from technical high school Vienna and a Master's in Law from the University of Vienna.


Rainier Gruber, Founder

Rainier has developed software applications ranging from realtime image recognition to transactional server systems. Over the last years, he worked on a distributed trading platform that processes thousands of transactions daily. At DiscreteLogics he focuses on data visualisation, it's relation to data storage and on software engineering practices in general.
